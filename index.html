About me

I was born and raised in Córdoba, Argentina, where I graduated as an Agricultural Engineer. I then pursued a Ph.D. at the University of Buenos Aires. At the end of 2022, I moved to the Bay Area and now work with individuals and businesses to provide plant solutions that enhance their spaces. My passion lies in bringing nature into everyday spaces and helping people cultivate thriving, green environments.
